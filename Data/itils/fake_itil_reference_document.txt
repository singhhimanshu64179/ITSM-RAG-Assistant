Title: ITIL Best Practices Reference

Incident Management:
- Goal is to restore service quickly.
- Functional escalation should follow a tiered model.
- Major Incidents require separate handling procedures.

Problem Management:
- Purpose: Prevent recurring incidents.
- Uses RCA techniques such as 5 Whys and Fishbone.

Change Management:
- Focus on minimizing business risks.
- Standard, Normal, Emergency change categories.

Service Desk:
- Acts as the single point of contact.
- Tracks incidents and service requests.

KPIs:
- MTTR, FCR, SLA Compliance, Change Success Rate
