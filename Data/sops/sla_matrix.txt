Title: SLA Matrix for Incident Management

SLA Targets Based on Priority:

Priority  | Response Time | Resolution Time
------------------------------------------------
P1 (Critical)  | 15 minutes      | 4 hours
P2 (High)      | 30 minutes      | 8 hours
P3 (Medium)    | 1 hour          | 24 hours
P4 (Low)       | 4 hours         | 72 hours

Escalation Rules:
- P1: Notify MIM immediately.
- P2: Escalate if no update in 60 minutes.
- P3: Escalate if not assigned in 4 hours.
- P4: Escalate if open for more than 24 hours.

Metrics Tracked:
- SLA Compliance %
- Number of SLA breaches
- Aging incidents
