Title: Service Desk L1 Responsibilities

Core Duties:
- First point of contact for users.
- Logging all incidents with proper category and CI.
- Initial troubleshooting.
- Applying known solutions from knowledge base.

Escalation Rules:
- Escalate to L2 if incident cannot be resolved within 30 minutes.
- Immediately escalate P1 incidents.

Communication:
- Provide updates every 30 minutes for P1, every 60 minutes for P2.

KPIs:
- First Call Resolution (FCR)
- Ticket logging accuracy
- Average Handling Time
