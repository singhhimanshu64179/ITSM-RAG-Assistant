Title: Service Desk L2 Responsibilities

Responsibilities:
- Advanced troubleshooting.
- Root cause identification.
- Apply patches and configuration changes (as per Change Management).
- Support L1 with knowledge articles.

Escalation:
- Escalate to L3 if technical expertise required.
- Notify MIM for potential major incidents.

KPIs:
- Mean Time to Resolve (MTTR)
- Backlog reduction
- Knowledge base contribution rate
