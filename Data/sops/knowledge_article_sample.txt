Title: Knowledge Article – VPN Connection Failure

Article ID: KA-00123
Category: Network → VPN
Audience: Service Desk L1 & End Users

Issue:
Users unable to connect to VPN. Error: "Authentication failed."

Cause:
Incorrect saved credentials or expired token.

Resolution Steps:
1. Open VPN Client > Settings.
2. Clear saved credentials.
3. Enter corporate ID manually.
4. Reset token from MFA app if expired.
5. Retry connection.

When to Escalate:
- Issue persists after 2 attempts.
- Affected user is part of critical teams.

Related Incidents:
INC1245, INC0912

Review Cycle:
Quarterly
