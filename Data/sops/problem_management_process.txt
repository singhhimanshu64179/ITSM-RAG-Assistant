Title: Problem Management Process (ITIL v4)

Purpose:
To prevent incidents from recurring and reduce impact of unavoidable incidents.

Process:
1. Problem Detection:
   - Identified via repeated incidents, trend analysis, or MIM.

2. Logging:
   - Problem ticket created with CI, description, business impact.

3. Categorization & Prioritization:
   - Based on impact, frequency, and severity.

4. Investigation & Diagnosis:
   - Perform RCA using methods such as:
     - 5 Whys
     - Fishbone Diagram
     - Kepner-Tregoe

5. Workaround:
   - Document temporary workaround in knowledge base.

6. Resolution:
   - Implement permanent fix via Change Management.

7. Closure:
   - Validate fix and update documentation.

KPIs:
- Reduction in repeat incidents
- Time to complete RCA
- Percentage of problems with documented workaround
