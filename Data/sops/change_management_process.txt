Title: Change Management Process (ITIL v4)

Purpose:
Ensure standardized methods for handling changes to minimize service risks.

Types of Changes:
- Standard: Pre-approved, low-risk.
- Normal: Requires approval (low, medium, or high risk).
- Emergency: Requires immediate implementation.

Process Steps:
1. Change Request Submission:
   - Includes justification, risk assessment, impacted CI, rollback plan.

2. Risk & Impact Analysis:
   - Change Manager + SMEs review technical impact.

3. Approval:
   - CAB approval required for medium/high-risk changes.

4. Implementation:
   - Execute change within scheduled window.

5. Review:
   - Validate success.
   - Update CMDB.

KPIs:
- Change success rate
- Number of emergency changes
- Failed change percentage
